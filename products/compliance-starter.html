Compliance Starter Kit

Sample SOPs, an audit-readiness checklist, and an editable SOP template to bootstrap good governance.

$97

Terms of Use

What’s inside

  • Audit-readiness checklist (DOC/PDF) with key controls and evidence list
  • 2–3 sample SOPs (DOC) for common processes (e.g., onboarding, cash handling)
  • Editable SOP template (DOC) — sections for purpose, scope, roles, steps, QA
  • Quick “policy vs. procedure” explainer and naming conventions

Who it’s for

  • Small teams that need documented processes to pass vendor or partner reviews
  • Founders preparing for growth, hiring, or certifications
  • Operators who want fewer mistakes and clearer accountability

How it works (quick path)

  1. Assess: run the checklist; mark what exists vs. gaps.
  2. Draft: copy the SOP template; adapt sample SOPs to your workflow.
  3. Implement: review with the team; assign owners and due dates.
  4. Prove: save evidence (logs, screenshots, training sign-offs) for audits.
