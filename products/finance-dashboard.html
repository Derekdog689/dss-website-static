Small Business Financial Dashboard

Excel/Google Sheets dashboard to track cashflow, use envelope budgeting, watch credit utilization, and view simple, decision-ready charts each month.

$67

Terms of Use

What’s inside

  • Cashflow register with month/quarter summaries
  • Envelope budgeting tab (set targets, track actuals)
  • Credit utilization view (per account + overall)
  • Charts tab (income vs. expense, category trends)
  • Setup & usage guide (PDF)

Who it’s for

  • Owners who want clarity on cash-in, cash-out, and runway
  • Solo/very small teams that prefer spreadsheets over apps
  • Anyone needing a simple, no-fluff dashboard to review monthly

How it works (3 steps)

  1. Set up: duplicate the template (Sheets) or open the Excel file; add starting balances & categories.
  2. Log activity: paste bank/CC exports or enter transactions as they occur.
  3. Review: check monthly charts, category variance, and utilization; adjust envelopes for next month.

Requirements & compatibility

  • Excel 2019+ or Microsoft 365 or Google Sheets
  • Works on Windows/Mac and mobile Sheets app
  • No macros, add-ins, or scripts required
